Format : MIDI, WAV
Quality : 24\16 bit 44.1 kHz stereo

Description : “Glacial” is the latest premium library from Origin Sound – a carefully selected collection of samples containing an entire sound universe populated by cascading melodies, sublime atmospheres, rich basslines, elegant chord progressions, effects and much more.

The cornerstone of this comprehensive library begins with professionally arranged drum loops. While these loops are carefully mixed and perfect for the mix, each drum loop comes as stems with percussion and treble drum sounds, as well as separate kick drum and snare grooves, giving you freedom when it comes to creating your rhythm. A variety of additional percussion loops are also included that can easily be combined with the rest of the stems. Drum loops provide great insight into how the arrangement of the 76 single drum samples included in the library was created.
In addition, “Glacial” provides a wide range of musical loops and single samples, covering all elements such as ethereal synths, unique melody lines, emotional chord progressions and signature basslines. Expect to hear instruments such as vintage synths, fluttering strings, gentle rhodos, moving guitars, cosmic vocals, ambient plucks and more. Each chord progression, bassline and melody also comes with a MIDI file, giving you the freedom to create your own sounds. 23 additional musical single samples are included for use in any samplers to effectively create your own instruments, or even for use as tonal effects throughout your mixes.

The effects presented in this library contain a range of characteristics: from organic build-ups, shimmering build-ups, to humming tones. These textures can be used in your arrangements to easily create transitions, smooth or even abrupt, or serve as an additional layer to your loops. Not to mention a large selection of bonus ambiences, which are especially useful when placed subtly in a mix as a supporting element for your crisp drum lines and warm bass lines.

“Glacial” is one of our largest libraries available with the highest audio quality. Ideal for those who want to create the illusion of a vast soundscape in their mixes.

Content :
•76 Warm drum hits
•10 Fluent drum loops (Full bounces and stem bounces)
•10 Extra perc loops
•20 Bonus lush atmospheres
•14 Thick bass Loops (including Midi files)
•17 Beautiful chord Loops (including Midi files)
•25 Atmospheric melody Loops (including Midi files)
•20 Foley infused SFX
•23 Musical one shots
